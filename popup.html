YouTube Citation Extension

This extension helps you create, manage, and share citations for YouTube videos. It adds a citation panel to the right side of YouTube videos.

Notice: You're not currently on a YouTube video page.

Go to a YouTube video to use the citation features.

üé¨Recording Video Segments

  1. Navigate to any YouTube video
  2. Look for the red record button in the video player controls next to the current time stamp
  3. Click it to start recording a segment
  4. Click it again when you reach the end of your segment
  5. A sidebar should appear and you can now click either add citation or add request to put the timestamp recorded into the citation or request

üìùAdding Citations

  1. Open the Citations tab in the extension panel
  2. Click "+ Add Citation"
  3. Fill out the form with:
    • Citation title
    • Timestamp range (e.g., 00:15:30)
    • Description
    • Source URL
  4. Choose to post anonymously or with your YouTube identity
  5. Submit your citation

üîçRequesting Citations

  1. Open the Citation Requests tab in the extension panel
  2. Click "+ Add Request"
  3. Specify the timestamp range and explain what needs citation in both the title and description
  4. Submit your request

Other users can view and respond to your citation requests.

üëçVoting on Citations

You can upvote (blue) or downvote (red) citations and requests to help the most accurate and helpful ones rise to the top.

üí°Responding to Requests

Click the "Respond" button on any citation request to create a citation that addresses it.
